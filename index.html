How long does it take to see tangible SEO results?
SEO is a long-term strategy. Typically, it takes between 3 to 6 months to start seeing significant improvements in rankings and organic traffic, depending on the competitiveness of your industry and your site's current standing.
Can you guarantee a #1 ranking on Google?
Nobody can guarantee a #1 ranking due to Google's constantly changing algorithms. However, I guarantee implementing the best data-driven practices that give your site the highest probability of ranking well and dominating your niche over time.
Do you offer White-Label SEO for Agencies?
Yes! I regularly partner with marketing and web-design agencies to provide white-labeled SEO services for their clients. Everything from audits to reporting is fully branded under your agency's name.
Will I receive monthly reports on my campaign?
Absolutely. Transparency is key. I provide comprehensive monthly reports detailing organic traffic growth, keyword ranking improvements, backlinks acquired, and a summary of tasks completed during the sprint.
Do you fix technical SEO issues or just content?
I cover the full spectrum. Every successful SEO campaign begins with a deep technical audit. I will fix site architecture, speed issues (Core Web Vitals), schema markup, and crawlability before scaling content.
Can you audit my existing website first?
Yes. A thorough audit is always step one. I evaluate your current on-page optimization, backlink profile, and technical health to identify exact bottlenecks holding your rankings back.
